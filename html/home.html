who we are

"Green Symphony" is the music festival of nature, which will be held for the first time on March 3, 2024 in the magnificent place of Maya Bay-Phi Phi Island, in Thailand, known to the general public for the film "The Beach". The Green Symphony aims to make its participants enjoy a unique and particular experience, between rainforest and wildlife, for those who love nature and music. The event will include the whole day, in fact the participants will be able to enjoy interesting activities during the day, and then make room for a real musical event in the evening.

morning activity

Daytime activities will include:
1. Snorkelling: If you are a fan of snorkelling, Phi Phi Island Lake is a great place to do it. There are many snorkelling spots along its shores, where you can see the rich marine life that inhabits the waters.
2. Stand-up puddleboarding: another particular activity on the lake. You can take a board and go around the lake in a fun and relaxing wav.
3. Excursions: If you like walking, there are some very charming green areas, where you can admire the local fauna.
4. Kavaking: Kavaking is a popular activity on Lake Phi Phi Island. You can take a kavak and enjoy a peaceful adventure amidst the crystal clear waters of the lake:

What artists
are there

In the evening, however, the concert will host artists whose music brings to mind images of natural landscapes, which reflects on nature and sustainability, or is simply inspired by the beauty of nature itself.

Ben Harper

his music is a fusion of blues, rock and reggae, he is often inspired by nature and its beauty

Jack Jahnson

known for relaxing acoustic music, often with lyrics reflecting on nature and the importance of protecting it

Xavier Rudd

his acoustic music incorporates elements of reggae and folk, among his most popular tunes known we find "Follow the Sun"

The Lumineers

known for relaxing acoustic music, often with lyrics reflecting on nature and the importance of protecting it

Eddie Edder

an artist with an extraordinary natural talent, he is known by most as the leader and frontman of Pearl Jam.Today he is a rock icon, a spokesperson together with a few other artists of the most beautiful and positive side of the genre that has revolutionized the history of music.

First Aid Kit

they are Swedish musical duo consisting of two sisters Johanna and Klara. The musical style is inspired by contemporary folk artists

Fleet Foxes

are an American folk rock band, formed in Seattle. Their lyrics are very accurate and have a lot of musical maturity
